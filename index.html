About Us


Founded in 2017, the Zimbabwe Australia Business Council is not for profit, non-partisan organisation.
The Council's member companies currently employ 1.7 million Zimbabweans and include thousands of businesses of all sizes. representing business leaders in every region and sector of Zimbabwe
